Why this matters for medical necessity

The Florida Keys are served by a single highway (US-1) with no alternate route; ground times rise sharply with traffic, weather, and bridge/road incidents. For time-sensitive conditions, rotor-wing transport provides the only timely access to tertiary trauma, neuro, cardiac, and critical-care resources in Miami.

Distances and times are planning estimates — confirm actual mileage and ETA with GPS / Google Maps for each transport. Ground times vary with traffic, weather, and road conditions; air times vary with winds, routing, and ATC.
